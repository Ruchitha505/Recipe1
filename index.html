Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 4 cups Water
  • 2-3 Green Chilies
  • tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • Spices (Turmeric, Garam Masala, etc.)
  • Onions, Garlic, Ginger
  • Fresh Coriander and Mint
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste.Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions till golden brown.
  3. Cook rice till 70% done.Drain and set aside.
  4. Heat oil and add spices, ginger-garlic paste, and add onions,tomatoes and cook till soft.
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook until partially done.
  6. Layer chicken and rice, and cook on low flame (dum).
